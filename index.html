Biography

I am a PhD student at Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Rama Chellappa . Before joining JHU, I received my Bachelor's and Master's Degree at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Weibei Dou. Previously, I interned at National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Yueming Jin.

My research interests include Computer Vision in 3D reconstruction and also robotic surgical data science. I recently work on unconstrained 3D reconstruction.
